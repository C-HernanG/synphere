# Code of Conduct

Synphere contributors are expected to be respectful, direct, and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- discuss ideas in good faith
- criticize code and decisions, not people
- keep review feedback specific and actionable
- respect privacy and security boundaries

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- doxxing or exposing private information
- abusive or intentionally inflammatory communication
- sharing secrets or private production material

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

Security-sensitive matters should not be raised in public threads. Use the private reporting path described in `SECURITY.md`.
